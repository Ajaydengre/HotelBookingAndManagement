CANCELLATION POLICY

First night payment for all booked rooms are non-refundable.

For cancellations done before more than 15 days prior to check in date: 20% of booking amount will be charged
For Cancellations done between 8 - 14 days prior to check in date: 50% of booking amount will be charged.
For cancellations done between 0-7 days prior to check in date: 100% of the booking amount will be charged.
No show policy: 100% of booking amount will be charged.

If a package is cancelled, the advance for the activities will be fully refunded but the above mentioned cancellation policy will be applied to the rooms and the the cancellation fee will be deducted from the advance paid for the package.

The no-show policy will be applied when the guest does not show up at the hotel within 24 hours of his stipulated check in time. In such a case, the reservation will be released.
Exceptions can be made if the guest establishes contact with the hotel management in writing (email) before the room is released.
It is up to Trip Inn if it wants to inform the guest when the room is released in a case of no show.
